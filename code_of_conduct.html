Code of Conduct

1. Professional Integrity

  • Members shall maintain honesty, integrity, and transparency in all professional activities.
  • Members must uphold the dignity and reputation of ISTE at all times.

2. Commitment to Education

  • Promote quality technical education and research.
  • Encourage innovation and continuous learning.

3. Ethical Behaviour

  • Avoid conflicts of interest.
  • Respect intellectual property rights.

4. Respect and Inclusiveness

  • Treat all members, students, and colleagues with respect.
  • Promote diversity and equal opportunity.

5. Social Responsibility

  • Contribute to societal development through education and technical advancement.
  • Support sustainable and ethical practices.
