ISTE Objectives

Empowering the Technical Education Ecosystem

Primary Objectives

  • To provide quality training programs to teachers and administrators of technical institutions.
  • To assist in developing professional engineers and technicians required by industry and organizations.
  • To provide guidance and training to students for better learning and personality development.
  • To enhance the status of the teaching profession and promote professional ethics.
  • To contribute to curriculum development and technical education policy.
  • To promote research and development in technical education.

Strategic Goals

The ISTE Telangana Section actively works to realize these objectives by fostering academic exchange, professional development programs, and meaningful collaboration between institutions and industry.
