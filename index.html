Welcome to ISTE Telangana Section

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national professional non-profit society registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860.

ISTE Telangana Section promotes innovation, research, academic excellence and professional development across engineering and technical institutions.

Through training programmes, faculty development initiatives, annual conventions and student activities, the Section strengthens technical education throughout the state.

We strive to build a collaborative platform for educators, institutions and students to innovate and grow together.
