Advancing Quality Technical Education Across Telangana

About ISTE

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading national professional, non-profit society dedicated to the advancement of technical education in India. Established in 1941 as the Association of Principals of Technical Institutions (APTI) and reconstituted as ISTE in 1968, the society has played a pivotal role in fostering excellence in technical teaching, learning, and research.

ISTE is registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860 and is committed to enhancing the quality and relevance of technical education for both faculty and students across the nation.

National Presence and Impact

With a diverse membership comprising thousands of professional teachers, student members, and institutional members including premier institutes of technology and engineering, ISTE serves as a cornerstone for educational development.

Formation of ISTE Telangana Section

The ISTE Telangana Section was established following the bifurcation of the erstwhile Andhra Pradesh Section, aligning with the vision and policies of the ISTE National Executive Council.

Our Vision and Commitment

The ISTE Telangana Section aspires to be a hub of academic excellence, fostering leadership, innovation and transformative educational practices.

Value for Students and Educators

By engaging with ISTE programs and networks, students gain exposure to industry trends and innovation-driven platforms, while faculty benefit from pedagogical training and research collaborations.

About ISTE Telangana

The ISTE Telangana Section works to promote quality technical education, innovation and professional growth among institutions and students.
